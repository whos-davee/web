MISSION

We develop socially competent and highly skilled college graduates from the poorest but brightest and most goal oriented in the slum, by paying their education in good schools and also training them in social and leadership skills in order to get the best of two worlds: The proactiveness in the successful from the slum combined with social and technical skills needed to make the class journey. These become hardworking, goal-oriented, and flexible professionals and entrepreneurs.

VISION

The Kff Foundation envisions empowering underprivileged yet talented youth in Cebu City to become future leaders and role models. By addressing environmental, cultural, and social challenges, the foundation nurtures their potential through comprehensive support, education, and leadership development.
The goal is to enable these individuals to break societal barriers, achieve professional excellence, and contribute to a prosperous Philippines. The foundation serves as an extended family, fostering resilience, character, and a network to ensure success while promoting sustainability for future generations.

HISTORY

The foundation started as a promise. A promise of commitment eventually grew and helped lots of young, determined, intelligent individuals of Cebu. The year was 1983 when a European went to the Philippines and chose Cebu as his point of destination.
He was a government doctor who specialized in the care and treatment of problem youths in parts of Europe. During his stay in the country, among many other people he met a boy. Inspired by the boy's dedication, he helped him continue his studies and fulfill his dreams. Through time, the family of scholars expanded and the promise went on in the name of Kff Foundation.
